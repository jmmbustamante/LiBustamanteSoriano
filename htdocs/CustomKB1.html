GamaKay LK67

With a tray mounted design and on-board stabilizers, the Gamakay LK67 comes with noise dampening foam between its PCB and board. It comes with a volume knob on the top-right, that also plays/pauses when pressed. Even with its plastic structure, it still feels very sturdy. Using Gateron yellows, it would allow the board to sound better than its stock, and that's just it being unlubed. Also using XDA keycaps, it wouldn't really affect the sound, but sure would affect its looks, as XDA keycaps look quite great for the average person. And lastly, its price, sitting at around ₱3,500 from shops like Lazada or Shopee, it's definetely worth the price.
